Keywords serve as the bedrock of SEO, and performing thorough keyword research is essential to comprehend your target audience's search intent and optimize your website accordingly. Begin by identifying pertinent keywords that are frequently searched by your target audience and have low competition. Utilize keyword research tools such as Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, or Ahrefs to discover high-volume, low-competition keywords to improve your Google ranking. After identifying target keywords, optimize your website's content, meta tags, headings, URLs, and other on-page elements with these keywords. Ensure that the keywords are used organically and do not appear overly promotional. Also, concentrate on producing high-quality, engaging, and informative content that offers value to your users and aligns with their search intent.
